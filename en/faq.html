Frequently Asked Questions

  • What projects are you most proud of?

    I'm proud of how HarmoniaUI came up. I think the code is organized and pretty clean, obviously there is room for improvement but I'm satisfied with it. There was a lot of struggle with HarmoniaUI, the documentation on making Godot modules was not extensive so I had to look in the Godot source code to figure out how to make some things.

  • What are your biggest strengths as a developer?

    I think my biggest strengths are that I'm passionate about development. I don't give up easily, I'm creative and which helps me come up with different ideas in games and other. I can adapt to many different frameworks, languages and technologies, I would say thats one of my biggest strength

  • What development tools and frameworks do you use regularly?

    Most often I use VSCode, Visual Studio, For game development either Unity or Godot, but I really wanna try Unreal. I usually use VSCode with plugins that color the texts or help develop faster.

  • Do you work with both front-end and back-end development?

    Yes, since a lot of my projects were done solo I had to do both front and back development. I have a lot of experience in UI/UX from developing different websites.

  • Do you specialize in 2D or 3D game development?

    Most of my projects are made in 2D, but I would want to develop some more 3D games.

  • Do you develop games for PC, console, or mobile platforms?

    Depends on the game, a lot of my games were made for PC only and some were for mobile and PC. Usually it's either PC or Mobile.

  • How do you design engaging game mechanics?

    I either brainstorm or look for inspiration in different games, I try to make it fun for players.

  • What game engines do you use?

    I mostly use Unity and Godot, but I really want to try Unreal soon.
